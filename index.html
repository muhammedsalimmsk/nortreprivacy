Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at Notre Pharmacies

Introduction

Notre Pharmacies is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your information. By using our app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with the best possible service:

  • Personal Information: Your name and email address during signup for user and shop accounts.
  • Shop Details: For shop owners, we collect shop information such as the shop's name, location, and contact details after authentication.
  • Location Data: We collect user location data to help find nearby pharmacies and improve the accuracy of our services.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services, ensuring users can easily locate nearby pharmacies.
  • To display shop details, including pictures and contact information, for user convenience.
  • To personalize the user experience based on location data.

We ensure that your data is used solely for these purposes and is handled responsibly.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our team regularly reviews and updates our security protocols to ensure your data remains safe. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Notre Pharmacies does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for any reason. All collected data is used exclusively for improving our services and providing you with relevant information.

Your Choices and Rights

As a user of Notre Pharmacies, you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Compliance

Notre Pharmacies is dedicated to adhering to relevant privacy laws and regulations. While we aim to comply with standards such as GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), we recommend that users review these laws for additional guidance. Please contact us if you have specific concerns or inquiries about compliance.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review our policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
